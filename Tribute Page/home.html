Arch Bishop Benson Idahosa

Tribute to his emminence Arch Bishop Benson Idahosa

Timeline

1938

Born 11 September 1938 Benin City, Edo, Nigeria

1968

Inaugurated the Church of God Mission International, which long started as a small prayer group.

1971

Commissioned into ministry by Pa Elton and James Gordon Lindsey.

1981

Ordained a Bishop, and later ordained many others.

1989

Ordained Bishop David Oyedepo of the Living Faith Church worldwide.

1998

Arch Bishop Benson Idahosa died on 12 March 1998.
